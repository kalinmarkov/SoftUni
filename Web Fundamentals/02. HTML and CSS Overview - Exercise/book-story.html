The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now. On her desc, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist, and all the others scientific journals her work had appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of On The Road. It had been Molly's favorite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under the poster with the Oscar Wilde quote proclaiming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do.
